Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

This extension is built to be privacy-clean by design.

What it does

It adds a "Download all" button to the Adobe Podcast / Enhance Speech web app so you can download your own enhanced files in one click instead of one at a time.

What data it touches

  • Your Adobe files: it reads the file list of your current Enhance Speech session — using the session you are already signed into — and only ever touches your own, authenticated files.
  • Settings: your rename-pattern preference is stored locally in chrome.storage on your device.

What it does NOT do

  • It does not store your Adobe credentials.
  • It does not send your files, file contents, or browsing data to any server we operate.
  • It does not bypass Adobe's download limits — it automates clicking, nothing else.
  • It does not track you.

Payment

Pro is a one-time purchase handled by ExtensionPay (Stripe). The only thing that leaves your browser is an anonymous license check confirming whether you have paid. Card details are handled entirely by ExtensionPay/Stripe and are never seen by this extension.

Permissions, and why

  • downloads — to save your files to disk.
  • storage — to remember your settings and license status.
  • scripting / activeTab / tabs — to detect files on the active Adobe tab.
  • host access to adobe.com — so the button can appear on Enhance Speech.
  • host access to extensionpay.com — for the license/payment check.
